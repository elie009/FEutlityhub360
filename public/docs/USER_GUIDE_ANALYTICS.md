## üéØ Financial Health Score

### What is Financial Health?
A comprehensive score that considers:
- **Savings Rate**: Percentage of income saved
- **Debt-to-Income Ratio**: Debt relative to income
- **Bill Payment History**: On-time payment rate
- **Emergency Fund**: Availability of emergency savings
- **Spending Stability**: Consistency of spending

### Score Ranges
- **90-100 (Excellent)**: Strong financial health
- **75-89 (Good)**: Solid financial position
- **60-74 (Fair)**: Room for improvement
- **Below 60 (Needs Attention)**: Requires immediate action

### Improving Your Score
1. **Increase Savings**: Save more each month
2. **Reduce Debt**: Pay down loans faster
3. **Pay Bills On Time**: Never miss payments
4. **Build Emergency Fund**: Maintain 3-6 months expenses
5. **Control Spending**: Keep expenses within income

## ‚ùì Common Questions

**Q: How often is the data updated?**
A: Analytics update in real-time as you record transactions, pay bills, and update accounts.

**Q: Can I customize the reports?**
A: You can select different time periods, categories, and accounts to focus on what matters to you.

**Q: How far back does the data go?**
A: All historical data from when you started using UtilityHub360 is available.

**Q: Can I compare multiple years?**
A: Yes! Use custom date ranges or select "Last Year" to compare with current year.

**Q: What if my financial health score is low?**
A: Review the specific factors affecting your score and focus on improving them. The score will update as you make improvements.

**Q: Can I export all my data?**
A: Yes! Use the export function to download comprehensive reports in various formats.

**Q: How accurate are the projections?**
A: Projections are based on historical trends and patterns. Accuracy improves with more data.

**Q: Can I set up alerts based on analytics?**
A: Yes! Set up notifications for spending thresholds, trends, or financial health changes in Settings.
