## üìñ What Are Transactions?

Transactions are records of money moving in or out of your accounts, including:
- **Income**: Salary, freelance payments, refunds, gifts
- **Expenses**: Purchases, bill payments, withdrawals
- **Transfers**: Moving money between accounts
- **Other**: Any financial activity

## üì± Viewing Your Transactions

### Navigate to Transactions
1. Go to **Finance ‚Üí Transactions** in the sidebar
2. You'll see a list of all your transactions

### Transaction List View
Each transaction shows:
- **Date**: When the transaction occurred
- **Description**: What the transaction was for
- **Amount**: How much money was involved
- **Category**: Income, Expense, Transfer, etc.
- **Account**: Which account it affected
- **Status**: Completed, Pending, Cancelled

## ‚ûï Adding a Transaction Manually

### Step 1: Click "Add Transaction"
1. On the Transactions page, click **"Add Transaction"**
2. Fill in the transaction form

### Step 2: Enter Details
- **Transaction Type**: 
  - Income (money coming in)
  - Expense (money going out)
  - Transfer (moving between accounts)
- **Amount**: The transaction amount
- **Date**: When it occurred (defaults to today)
- **Description**: What this transaction was for
- **Category**: 
  - Food & Dining
  - Shopping
  - Transportation
  - Bills & Utilities
  - Entertainment
  - Income
  - Transfer
  - Others
- **Account**: Select the bank account affected
- **Tags**: Optional labels (e.g., "business", "personal")
- **Notes**: Any additional information

### Step 3: Save
Click **"Save"** to add the transaction.

## üìÖ Transaction Statuses

- Transaction is finalized

- Most common status

### Pending (‚è≥)
- Transaction is initiated but not yet completed
- Common for transfers or scheduled payments

### Cancelled (‚ùå)
- Transaction was cancelled
- Does not affect account balance

## ‚ùì Common Questions

**Q: Can I import transactions from my bank?**
A: Yes, if you connect your bank accounts, transactions can be imported automatically. Check Bank Accounts settings.

**Q: How do I handle split transactions?**
A: Create separate transactions for each part, or add notes explaining the split.

**Q: What if I make a mistake?**
A: Simply edit the transaction to fix it, or delete it if it was added by mistake.

**Q: How far back can I go when adding transactions?**
A: You can add transactions for any date - past or future (for scheduled transactions).

**Q: Can I export my transactions?**
A: Yes! Use the Reports & Analytics section to export transaction data.

**Q: Do transactions affect my account balance?**
A: Yes, completed transactions automatically update your account balances.

**Q: How do transfers between accounts work?**
A: Create a transfer transaction linking both accounts. The money moves but doesn't affect your total balance.
