## â“ Common Questions

**Q: Can I track multiple loans?**
A: Yes! Add all your loans to get a complete debt picture.

**Q: What if I refinance a loan?**
A: Update the loan with new terms (rate, payment, balance) or create a new loan entry and mark the old one as paid off.

**Q: How do I handle loans with variable interest rates?**
A: Update the interest rate when it changes. The payment schedule will adjust accordingly.

**Q: Can I see how much interest I've paid?**
A: Yes! Check the loan details and payment schedule for total interest paid.

**Q: What if I miss a payment?**
A: Mark it as overdue, then mark as paid when you make the payment. Update the payment schedule accordingly.

**Q: How do I track a paid-off loan?**
A: Mark the loan as "Paid Off" or "Completed". You can archive it or delete it.

**Q: Can I see my total debt across all loans?**
A: Yes! The Loans dashboard shows total debt across all loans.

**Q: What if my payment amount changes?**
A: Update the loan information with the new payment amount. Future payments will reflect the change.
