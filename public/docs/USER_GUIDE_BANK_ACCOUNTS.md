## üìö Understanding Bank Accounts in UtilityHub360

You can track multiple types of accounts:

## ‚ûï Adding a Bank Account

### Step 1: Navigate to Bank Accounts
1. Go to **Finance ‚Üí Bank Accounts** in the sidebar
2. You'll see all your connected accounts

### Step 2: Add Account
1. Click **"Add Account"** button
2. Fill in the account information:

#### Required Information:
- **Account Name**: e.g., "Main Checking", "Chase Credit Card"
- **Account Type**: 
  - Bank (checking/savings)
  - Credit Card
  - Investment
  - Savings
  - Other
- **Bank/Institution**: The name of your bank or financial institution
- **Account Number**: (Optional but recommended for tracking)
- **Current Balance**: Your current account balance

#### Optional Information:
- **Routing Number**: Bank routing number (for US accounts)
- **IBAN**: International Bank Account Number (for international accounts)
- **SWIFT Code**: For international transfers
- **Currency**: The currency for this account
- **Account Status**: Active or Inactive
- **Notes**: Any additional information

### Step 3: Save
Click **"Save"** or **"Create Account"** to add it.

## üîÑ Connecting External Accounts

For automatic transaction sync:

1. When adding an account, look for **"Connect Account"** option
2. Follow the connection wizard:
   - Enter your banking credentials (securely)
   - Grant permissions for read-only access
   - Confirm connection

**Benefits of connecting:**
- Automatic transaction import
- Real-time balance updates
- Better financial tracking

**Security Note:** UtilityHub360 uses bank-level security. Your credentials are encrypted and stored securely.

1. Find the account you want to edit

## üîÑ Syncing Account Data

### Manual Sync
1. Find the account
2. Click **"Sync Now"** or the sync icon
3. Wait for sync to complete

### Automatic Sync
- Set sync frequency in account settings:
  - Daily
  - Weekly
  - Monthly
  - Manual only

**Tip:** More frequent syncing provides more up-to-date information but may use more resources.

## üìä Viewing Account Details

Click on any account to see:
- **Account Overview**: Basic information and current balance

- **Balance Trends**: Chart showing balance over time

## üîå Connecting and Disconnecting Accounts

### Connect Account
1. Find an account that's not connected
2. Click **"Connect"**
3. Follow the connection process
4. Account will sync automatically

### Disconnect Account
1. Find the connected account
2. Click **"Disconnect"**
3. Confirm disconnection
4. Account remains in system but stops syncing

**Note:** Disconnecting doesn't delete the account or its transaction history.

## üóëÔ∏è Deleting an Account

**Warning:** This action permanently removes the account and all its data.

1. Find the account
2. Click **"Delete"** or trash icon
3. Confirm deletion

**When to delete:**
- Account is closed
- Duplicate account
- No longer relevant

**Alternative:** Consider marking account as "Inactive" instead of deleting.

## ‚ùì Common Questions

**Q: How many accounts can I add?**
A: There's no limit! Add as many accounts as you need.

**Q: Is my banking information secure?**
A: Yes! UtilityHub360 uses bank-level encryption and security. Your credentials are never shared.

**Q: What if I can't connect my bank?**
A: You can still add accounts manually and enter transactions. Connection is optional but convenient.

**Q: Can I hide accounts without deleting them?**
A: Yes! Mark them as "Inactive" - they'll remain but won't appear in main views.

**Q: How often should I sync accounts?**
A: Daily is recommended for most active accounts. Weekly or monthly works for less active accounts.

**Q: What if my balance is wrong?**
A: Manually update the balance, or check sync status. Sometimes syncing again fixes discrepancies.

**Q: Can I export account data?**
A: Yes! Use Reports & Analytics to export account information and transactions.
