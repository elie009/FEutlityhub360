## ‚ùì Common Questions

**Q: What if my income varies month to month?**
A: Use your average monthly income, or run the apportioner with your actual income each month.

**Q: Can I exclude certain bills from the calculation?**
A: The Apportioner includes all active bills. Mark bills as inactive if you don't want them included.

**Q: What if I don't have enough to cover everything?**
A: The apportioner will show a warning. Prioritize essential bills and loans, reduce savings temporarily, or find ways to increase income.

**Q: How often should I use the Apportioner?**
A: Review monthly at minimum, or whenever your income or expenses change significantly.

**Q: Can I save the allocation plan?**
A: The Apportioner reflects your current bills, loans, and savings. It automatically updates when these change.

**Q: What if I want to allocate more to savings?**
A: Increase your savings goal amounts, and the Apportioner will allocate accordingly (if income allows).

**Q: How does it handle annual bills?**
A: Annual bills are divided by 12 to show monthly allocation. Make sure annual bills are properly set up in the Bills section.

**Q: Can I use the Apportioner for budgeting future months?**
A: Yes! Use projected income and bills to plan ahead.
